Milk Products & Health Benefits

Product 1: Yogurt

Yogurt is a rich source of probiotics, which are beneficial for digestive health. It also helps boost the immune system.

Product 2: Cheese

Cheese is high in calcium and protein, essential for strong bones and muscles. It's also a good source of vitamins A and B12.

Product 3: Butter

Butter contains healthy fats and fat-soluble vitamins. It can help improve cholesterol levels when consumed in moderation.

Product 4: Ice Cream

Ice cream provides a quick source of energy and contains calcium. Enjoy it occasionally for a sweet treat!

Product 5: Ghee

Ghee is a form of clarified butter that is rich in nutrients. It's good for digestion and has anti-inflammatory properties.
